1. Introduction

Lufio is a mobile application designed to help users improve their well-being through guided breathing exercises. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to maintaining a minimal and transparent approach to data handling.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Lufio does not require an account, does not track usage, and does not connect to external analytics services.

Specifically:

  • No names, emails, or passwords are collected.
  • No usage or behavioral data is tracked.
  • No third-party SDKs (e.g., analytics, ads, social media) are integrated.
  • All app activity and preferences remain entirely on your device.

3. Internet & Connectivity

Lufio works offline. The app does not connect to the internet or external servers except when:

  • You manually open an external link (e.g., app support or website).
  • The App Store system handles updates or purchases (if applicable in the future).

4. In-App Purchases (future)

Lufio may introduce Pro features through in-app purchases in the future. These purchases are managed entirely by Apple’s App Store infrastructure. Lufio does not access or store any billing information.

5. Children’s Privacy

Lufio is safe for all ages. No personal information is collected from anyone, including children under 13, in compliance with COPPA and other applicable privacy regulations.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect app changes. If so, we will post the updated version here and adjust the “Last updated” date above.
